Pesto & goat's cheese risotto

  • olive oil
  • 200g risotto rice
  • 700ml chicken stock
  • 1 tub fresh pesto
  • 100g soft goat's cheese

Methods

Step 1

Pour a glug of olive oil into a large saucepan. Tip in the rice and fry for 1 min.
Add half the stock and cook until absorbed. Add the remaining stock, a ladle at a time,
and cook until the rice is al dente, stirring continually, for 20-25 mins.
